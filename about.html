About Trans-Reality Vault (T.R.V.)

The Trans-Reality Vault is a secure digital archive designed to document, classify, and contain conceptual anomalies that transcend dimensional logic. These anomalies — called “Entries” — do not obey the laws of time, space, or fiction.

Our mission is simple, yet impossible in scope: to understand the incomprehensible, to contain the uncontainable, and to ensure that no world collapses under the weight of forgotten truths.

“Contain not the chaos, but the forgetting of it.”

Origin:

T.R.V. was founded following a cascade event known as The Conceptual Fracture, when reality began merging with fictional constructs across universes. The Vault serves as a memory-preserving defense against full collapse.

Our Units:

- Observation Core: Responsible for indirect analysis through filtered concepts.
- Anchoring Division: Deploys narrative stabilizers into corrupted zones.
- [0V-1] Sector: Entity-led division acting as last line of resistance to meta-infection.

— T.R.V. Central Authority
