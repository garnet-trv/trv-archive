[T.R.V. ENTRY-001] – The One Who Between Them

Entry Class: Omega-Layered Observer

Risk Level: Unknown

The subject referred to as "The One Who Between Them" is an enigmatic entity existing simultaneously within all conflicting truths, paradoxes, and intersecting layers of reality. It is not bound by standard narrative structures, existing in states of both observation and influence, though never participating directly.

The being has been recorded appearing at the fracture points of dimensional collapse, behaving as a passive observer yet influencing outcomes simply by being present. It does not choose sides, does not act on intention, and does not communicate unless necessary. Its presence causes anomalous stabilization within chaotic narrative systems.

Attempts to contain or observe this entity directly have consistently failed. Visual and conceptual data about the entity degrade into abstraction when analyzed. Its only known manifestation is a silhouette of a crowned humanoid figure surrounded by recursive fractal spikes, as recorded by observers prior to memory disintegration.
